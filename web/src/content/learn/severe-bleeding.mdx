# Severe bleeding — first steps

**Call emergency services** (or direct someone else to call). Wear gloves if available; protect yourself first.

## Direct pressure

- Apply **firm, direct pressure** over the bleeding point with a clean cloth or dressing.
- **Do not remove** material that becomes soaked — add more on top and keep pressure.

## Limb injuries

- If bleeding is on an arm or leg and life-threatening bleeding continues after direct pressure, a **tourniquet** placed **high and tight** on the limb (per training) may be indicated where protocols allow — **follow local guidance and training**.

## Shock

- Help the person lie down, **keep them warm**, and reassure them.
- Monitor **level of responsiveness** and breathing until professionals arrive.
